Our Facilities

Provide First Class Facilities and Vehicle Maintenance support for 68 buildings on the main campus - in excess of 2M Square Feet and a fleet of 160 vehicles; Unparalleled Campus support for 6,000 students, faculty and staff; and Outstanding Grounds Maintenance support for a 300 Acre campus (Main Campus).

World Class Library

The Johnston Memorial Library advances the academic success of students, faculty, staff, and research professionals. Our facility offers an extensive print collection, full-text digital periodicals, and other digital materials to support the academic work of our University community.
